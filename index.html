Your lunch break, reimagined.

In need of some intelligent conversation over lunch or your coffee break? Waddle is just the app you need! With the tap of a button, find a lunch or coffee buddy withing walking distance, instantly!

How it works

Simply download the app, create an account, and choose coffee or lunch when you're ready to eat! We'll put you in touch with somebody nearby who's also currently availabe. From there you can use the chat functionality to find one antoher. Once you've arrived, you'll get a picture of that person to help you find them in a crowded area.
